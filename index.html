Hello!

I am a fifth- and final-year Ph.D. candidate at Carnegie Mellon University where I am part of the Theory Group and am fortunate to be advised by Fatma Kilinc-Karzan.

My research interests lie in the development and application of convex optimization tools towards nonconvex problems. My work on exactness within SDP relaxations of QCQPs was recognized with the INFORMS Optimization Society 2021 Best Student Paper Award.
