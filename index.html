About

Hello! I am a fifth- and final-year Ph.D. candidate at Carnegie Mellon University where I am part of the Theory Group and am fortunate to be advised by Fatma Kilinc-Karzan. Previously, I graduated summa cum laude from Northwestern University in 2017, with a B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science.

My research interests lie in the development and application of convex optimization tools towards nonconvex problems. In this direction, I have published several papers in top-tier optimization venues such as Math Programming, Math of OR, IPCO, and INFORMS TutORials. My work on exactness within SDP relaxations of QCQPs was recently recognized with the INFORMS Optimization Society 2021 Best Student Paper Award.
