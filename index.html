About

Hello! I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at CMU (where I am part of the Theory Group) and am fortunate to be advised by Fatma Kılınç-Karzan. Previously, I graduated from Northwestern University in 2017 with a B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics.

My research interests lies broadly in continuous optimization. Recently, my work has focused on nonconvex quadratically constrained quadratic programs and their semidefinite program relaxations.
