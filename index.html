About

Hello! I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at CMU (where I am part of the Theory Group) and am fortunate to be advised by Fatma Kılınç-Karzan. Previously, I graduated from Northwestern University in 2017 with a B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics.

My research interests lie at the intersection of continuous optimization and theoretical computer science. Recently, my work has focused on nonconvex quadratically constrained quadratic programs and their semidefinite program relaxations. My contributions in this area have been recognized by the INFORMS Optimization Society’s 2021 Student Paper Prize.
