About Me

I am a third year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University where I am part of the Theory Group. I am fortunate to be advised by Fatma Kılınç-Karzan. Previously, I obtained my undergraduate degree at Northwestern University where I was a computer science and math major.

Research Interests

My research interests lie broadly at the intersection of theoretical computer science and continuous optimization. I have recently been thinking about quadratically constrained quadratic programs and other problems in convex and nonconvex optimization.
